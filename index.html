About Me

I'm originally from Ventura, California and grew up in the Willamette Valley, just north of Salem, Oregon. I studied Applied Linguistics, Ecology, and Philosophy at Chemeketa College. After college, I started working as a guitar instructor for 2 years and then as a cook in various "fine-ish" dining restaurants in Salem and Portland, Oregon for about 6 years. I then spent a year working for a nonprofit that surveys timber sales in Mt. Hood National Forest. After experiencing nonprofit and union organizing burnout, I decided to change gears and seek a change of career. A friend of mine who works in tech invited me to a tech meetup. It was there that I learned about Epicodus and decided to enroll.
